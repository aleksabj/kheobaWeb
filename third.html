CRM System and Features

We have our CRM system located at kheoba.com

Tour Management Dashboard

  • Tour Overview: Get a snapshot of all your upcoming tours, including dates, locations, and available spots.
  • Booking Management: Easily view and manage all bookings, update availability, and communicate with customers.
  • Tour Customization: Add detailed descriptions, upload photos, and provide important information about each tour.

Personalized Assistant Interface

  • Schedule Management: Keep track of all your tours and appointments in one place.
  • Automated Responses: Set up automatic replies to common customer inquiries, saving you time and ensuring quick responses.
  • Customization Options: Tailor the assistant to match your style and preferences.

Analytics and Reporting Page

  • Performance Metrics: Track key metrics like number of bookings, customer satisfaction scores, and revenue.
  • Detailed Reports: Generate reports that provide insights into your business performance, helping you make informed decisions.
  • Customer Feedback: View and analyze customer reviews to continuously improve your services.
